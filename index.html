Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet in our solar system, orbiting the Sun at an average distance of 57.91 million kilometers (0.387098 AU)
It is a terrestrial planet, with a rocky composition and a relatively small size, measuring approximately 4,879 kilometers (3,031 miles) in diameter.
